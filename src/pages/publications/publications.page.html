Books

Farkas, G. (2016). Mastering OpenLayers 3. Packt Publishing Ltd. 285 p.

This book was my first serious contribution to IT literature. The syntax, the examples, and all the code are heavily outdated. The principles and the philosophy behind OpenLayers discussed in this book are still sound, though.

Farkas, G. (2017). Practical GIS. Packt Publishing Ltd. 372 p.

As usual, I accumulated, synthesized, and distilled all the teaching, research, and business experience I had at the time about good GIS practices. This book can still be relevant if you are open to extending your open-source GIS toolkit beyond QGIS and willing to put some energy into it.

I might have some excess digital copies if you ask nicely.
